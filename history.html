History

The LENK Group can look back on a
history of more than 100 years.

1900

Foundation as Papierfabrik Kappelrodeck

In 1900 we were originally founded as Papierfabrik Kappelrodeck with its first paper mill in Kappelrodeck.

1918

Invention of crepe paper

In 1918 we invented the crepe paper and applied for a patent. It was the first patent of many to come resulting from our constant research and development of new customized specialty paper.

2011

PM17 in Titisee joins LENK

In 2011 we welcomed a new member to our LENK family. PM17 in Titisee-Neustadt joined us from Schoeller Technocell and increased our annual production capactiy by 18,000 to 20,000 tons of specialty paper.

2019

Now LENK Paper GmbH

In 2019 we were acquired by our current owner, the private, canadian investor Mr. Rajeev Singh and we then became LENK Paper GmbH.

2021

PM1 in Bad Dürkheim joins LENK

In 2021 we welcomed another member to our LENK family. The paper mill Schleipen in Bad Dürkheim, a leader in book printing paper, now complements the production capacity with another 30,000 tons of high quality and specialty paper a year.

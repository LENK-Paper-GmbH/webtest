Privacy / Data protection

General information
Thank you for your visit and the interest in our company. LENK Paper GmbH attaches great importance to the protection of your privacy and your data. The following notes provide an overview of how we collect your personal data and process it in accordance with the EU GDPR and the data protection provisions of the Federal Republic of Germany. Personal data is any data that personally identifies you.

Data Collection
Your data will be collected on the one hand, by telling us this. This may be, for example, data that you enter in a contact form, or send us an e-mail to contact us.
Other data is collected automatically when visiting the website through our IT systems. These are above all technical data (eg Internet browser, operating system or time of the page request). The collection of this data is automatic and anonymous as soon as you enter our website.

Use and storage of the data
If you contact us by e-mail, telephone or fax, your request, including all personal data resulting from it, will be stored and processed for the purpose of processing your request and the associated technical administration.
The processing of this data is based on Art. 6 (1) (b) GDPR, provided that your request is related to the performance of a contract or is required to carry out pre-contractual measures. In all other cases, the processing is based on your consent (Article 6 (1) a DSGVO) and / or on our legitimate interest in the effective processing of the requests addressed to us (Article 6 (1) (f) GDPR) ,
The data collected automatically when you visit the website is used for statistical purposes or to analyze your surfing behaviour. The process is also technically necessary so that we can show you the website optimally. A transfer or other use of this data does not take place.

Information about your data
You always have the right to obtain free information about the origin, recipient and purpose of your stored personal data (Art. 15 GDPR). You also have the right to demand the publication, correction, forwarding, blocking or deletion of this data (Art. 16-20 GDPR). Furthermore, you have a right of appeal to the competent supervisory authority.
In addition, you have the right to request the restriction of the processing of your personal data in certain circumstances. In addition, you can revoke existing consent pursuant to Art. 7 para. 3 DSGVO with effect for the future. In the case of withdrawal, we will delete the data concerned immediately, as far as further processing can not be based on a legal basis for consentless processing. The revocation of consent does not affect the legality of the processing carried out on the basis of the consent until the revocation.
